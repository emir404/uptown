## Party service / catering (`partyservice.html`)

UPTOWN caters events either at the customer's home or in the restaurant.

- **In-house events / seminars:** up to **60 people** — Taufen (baptisms), Hochzeiten
  (weddings), Konfirmationen (confirmations), Weihnachtsfeiern (Christmas parties),
  Geburtstagsfeiern (birthday parties) and Seminare (seminars).
- **Off-site party service (with delivery):** up to **50 people**.
- They provide homemade specialities plus matching drinks, tailored to the customer's wishes.
- **To book:** Phone 0451 / 707 95 65, email uptown@t-online.de, or in person at
  Kronsforder Allee 3a, 23560 Lübeck.

## House rules & policies (from the placemats)

- **Payment accepted:** MasterCard, VISA ("WE ACCEPT – WIR AKZEPTIEREN").
- **No vaping:** "DAMPFEN NICHT GESTATTET".
- **Menu changes:** each change to a dish is charged at **+1,50 €** ("Jede Änderungen berechnen wir mit 1,50").
- **Half portions:** charged at **2/3 of the price** ("Halbe Portion berechnen wir mit 2/3 des Preises").
- **Sharing / extra cover:** "Gerichte teilen oder extra Gedeck **2,00 €**".
- **Doggy bag:** leftovers packed on request for a **1,00 €** packaging fee. **No doggy bag for
  "All you can eat"** dishes.
- **Allergens/additives:** a list of allergens and additives is available on request — ask staff.
