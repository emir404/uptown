## Wochenkarte — Weekly / seasonal special: Fresh Plaice & Matjes

From the `Scholle 2026` placemat (the site's **Wochenkarte** page and homepage both show it).
Reference image: [`reference-images/weekly-special-scholle.png`](reference-images/weekly-special-scholle.png)

### Große frische Scholle — 23,50 €
Large fresh plaice, pan-fried in butter ("in Butter gebraten"), served with lemon and
cucumber salad. Choice of homemade **Kartoffelsalat** (potato salad) **or Röstkartoffeln**
(fried potatoes).
- mit extra Speckstippe (bacon gravy) — **24,50 €**
- mit extra Speckstippe und Nordseekrabben 50 g (North Sea shrimp) — **26,50 €**

### Echte holländische Matjesfilets — real Dutch matjes herring fillets
Served with Röstkartoffeln.
- serviert mit roten Zwiebeln und Preiselbeer-Senf (red onions, cranberry mustard) — **14,50 €**
- serviert mit klassischer Hausfrauensauce mit Äpfel, Zwiebeln, Gurken in Sahnesauce — **16,50 €**

## Smoke-Town-Ribs — "All you can eat"

From `aktionen.html` and the ribs poster.

- **"Best Ribs in Town" — Smoke-Town-Ribs, Rippen satt (all you can eat)**
- **When:** every **Friday and Saturday** ("jeden Freitag und Samstag")
- **Price:** **23,50 € p.P.** (poster shows €23,50 p.P.; matches the "Smoke-Town-Ribs satt" line on the food menu)
- No voucher redemption ("Keine Gutschein Einlösung möglich"). No doggy bag on all-you-can-eat.

## Scampi "satt" — all-you-can-eat prawns

From the `Scampi 2026` poster (current) and `aktionen.html`.

- **When:** the **first Tuesday of every month** ("jeden ersten Dienstag im Monat").
- **What:** for two hours, as many spicy Riesengarnelen (prawns, peeled & deveined) as you can
  eat, with a bread mix + three homemade dips and a salad plate with Sylter dressing.
- **Price:** **32,50 € p.P.** *(2026 poster. The older 2024 placemat listed 29,50 € — use 32,50 €.)*
- **Booking:** advance registration **and deposit required** ("Anmeldung und Anzahlung
  erforderlich"), prepayment only ("Nur mit Vorkasse"). No voucher redemption.
- **2026 dates (first Tuesdays):** 3 Feb · 3 Mar · 7 Apr · 5 May · 2 Jun · 7 Jul ·
  4 Aug · 1 Sep · 6 Oct · 3 Nov · 1 Dec.

## Caipi-Donnerstag — Caipirinha Thursday

From `aktionen.html` and the drinks placemat.

- **When:** every **Thursday from 20:00** ("jeden Donnerstag ab 20.00 Uhr").
- **Deal:** Caipirinha for just **5,00 €** (normal Caipirinha price is 6,50 €).
